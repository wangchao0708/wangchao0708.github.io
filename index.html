About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in School of Cyber Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), supervised by Prof. Weiming Zhang and Prof. Nenghai Yu. I also collaborate closely with Dr. Kejiang Chen. Before that, I got my B.Eng. in Information Security from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2021.

My research interest is Information Hiding and AI Security. Recently, I mainly focus on Steganography.
